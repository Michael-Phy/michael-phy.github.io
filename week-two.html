Weekly Lab Notes & Work

2D and 3D Design & Laser Cutting Training

This week’s core learning content covered laser cutting cardboard operations and 2D/3D drawing design in Fusion 360. I first completed the lab required task: modeling and fabricating the useless box. During this process, I practiced sketch creation, part assembly, exporting DXF files, and distinguishing cutting lines and indentation score lines for folding cardboard structures.

After finishing the standard useless box exercise, I designed a personalized small storage box for holding electronic components on my own. The box adopts a heptagonal seven-sided structure, matching the seven letters in my name MICHAEL. Each side of the heptagon can be engraved with one letter from M, I, C, H, A, E, L separately, combining personalized design with practical storage functions for the electronic parts needed in my final project.
