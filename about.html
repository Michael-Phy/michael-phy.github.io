Self Introduction

My name is Michael Jia Dong Ho, a student in Harvard Summer PHYS S-12 Introduction to Digital Fabrication course. I am deeply interested in digital manufacturing, 3D printing, electronic circuit soldering and CNC machining. Throughout this course, I aim to master the full workflow from computer parametric modeling to physical fabrication, and eventually build a complete creative project integrating mechanical structures, electronic sensors and microcontroller programming.

I enjoy hands-on lab work, circuit assembly and design iteration. This website will record all my lab safety training, weekly fabrication experiments and final project progress for the whole semester.
