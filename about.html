Self Introduction

My name is Michael Jia Dong Ho. I am a high school student attending Harvard’s summer secondary school program. I really love physics and engineering.

Before this class, I only learned lots of theoretical knowledge from books. I rarely got the chance to turn these ideas into real physical objects. I hope to use all the theories I have learned to make useful tools and help people in real life.

This digital fabrication course lets me build physical products by myself. I want to combine my book knowledge with hands-on making. My final project is designed to help people with Parkinson’s disease, which is one way I can use engineering theories to bring convenience to others.

I will record all lab practices, safety training and my project progress on this website for the whole summer course.
