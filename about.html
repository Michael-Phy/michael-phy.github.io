Self Introduction

My name is Michael Jia Dong Ho. I am a high school student attending Harvard’s summer secondary school program. I have a strong passion for physics and engineering, and I plan to pursue related majors when I enter university in the future.

I enrolled in this Introduction to Digital Fabrication course to systematically master the full physical creation workflow, including 3D design & printing, laser cutting, CNC milling, circuit soldering, sensor electronics and microcontroller programming. I hope to combine theoretical physics knowledge with hands-on manufacturing skills through weekly lab practices.

This website will record all my lab safety training, weekly fabrication experiments, iteration processes and my final project design throughout the summer course.
